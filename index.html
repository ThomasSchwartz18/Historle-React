Welcome to Historle

Historle is a daily historical trivia game that challenges you to identify significant events from the past based on a series of carefully crafted clues. Each day, a new moment in human history becomes the focus of your guessing adventure. Whether it’s a turning point in politics, a groundbreaking scientific discovery, a pivotal cultural moment, or a milestone in exploration, Historle brings the past to life.

As a user, you can sign up for an account to track your performance over time. Your profile records your current win streak, total wins, days played, and longest streak, allowing you to monitor your progress and compare results with friends. Guests can also play without registering, but stats are only saved when you’re logged in.

On the home page, simply enter your guess based on the clues provided. You have multiple chances to refine your answer, with additional hints revealed after each attempt. When you’re ready, submit your final guess to see if you’re correct and unlock a detailed summary of the event. Challenge your friends by sharing your results or linking to our leaderboard, where top performers are showcased daily.

Historle was built with modern web technologies for a fast, responsive experience. The application is powered by React and Supabase, ensuring secure authentication, persistent data storage, and real-time updates. Our intuitive interface adapts to any device, from desktop to mobile, so you can enjoy Historle on the go.

We value your privacy and security. All profile data, including usernames and social handles, are stored in a secure database. You can delete your account at any time from the settings page. No personal information is shared with third parties, and we adhere to best practices to protect your data.

Thank you for playing Historle. We hope you’ll learn something new each day and discover fascinating stories from our collective past. If you have feedback or suggestions, please visit the Articles page to read our blog posts or contact us through the feedback link. Dive in and explore history, one clue at a time!
